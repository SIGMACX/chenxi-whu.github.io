About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Wuhan University. My research focuses on medical image analysis, medical world models, and intelligent analysis of fetal medical images, with particular interests in ultrasound imaging, segmentation, landmark detection, and multimodal learning.
